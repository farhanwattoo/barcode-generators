Next-Generation Barcode Migration and 2D Retail Readiness

This section covers the shift from single-purpose linear product codes toward richer 2D carriers that support both point-of-sale processing and digital experiences. The centerpiece is GS1 Sunrise 2027 planning, supported by practical guidance for Digital Link structure, packaging redesign, scanner readiness, and longer-term traceability strategy.

Important framing: GS1 Sunrise 2027 is an industry transition goal for the end of 2027, not an overnight switch-off date for legacy UPC or EAN labels. The strongest migration plans assume a phased period where both 1D and 2D workflows coexist.
